Hello,

So I have now the sufficient infrastructure to produce the packages for
the following distros (thanks to qemu - http://www.qemu.org):

RedHat 7.3 & 9, RedHat Enterprise Linux 3 & 4, Mandriva 10.1, 10.2,
2006, Fedora Core 4 & 5, SuSE Linux Enterprise Server 9 for i386.

All these packages are now available on ftp://ftp.mondorescue.org

I'll now work on a new revision of 2.0.7 to fix two annoying bugs found
after release, and will publish them hopefully this week. So SVN will ne
unfreezed to allow those modifications.

Roadmap for 2.0.8 then contains:
	- Add other virtual machines (SuSE 9.3 and 10), Debian 3.1 (yes
	  Andree ;-)
	- externalize dependencies around most of the fixed parameters in
	  mondo/mindi
	- support LVMv2/DM
	- fix the bugs reported in the BTS.

Feel free to give feedback !
