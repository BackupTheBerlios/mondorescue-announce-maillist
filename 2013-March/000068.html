Project mondorescue version 3.0.3 is now available

The project team is happy to announce the availability of a newest version of
mondorescue 3.0.3. Enjoy it as usual!

More details, as usual, in the logs at the end of this announce, and also on
the blog article at http://brunocornec.wordpress.com/2013/03/17/
mondorescue-3-0-3-is-finally-out/ which also asks for feedbacks from you around
the next steps.

Now available at ftp://ftp.mondorescue.org.
